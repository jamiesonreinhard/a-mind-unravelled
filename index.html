Hi, I'm Ashley

My friends call me Sabido (that's my last name). Or Ash. Or sometimes my family calls me Beans. I’ll basically answer to anything. I’m 27 years old. I’m originally from Louisiana and got my undergrad in psychology from LSU (GEAUX TIGAHS). I received my masters in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from the University of Cincinnati in May of 2019. Right now I live in Vail, Colorado, and am providing crisis therapy in a co-responder program with police and paramedics! I work in emergency departments, jails, and schools as well as the community, responding to various mental health crises. Before this I worked with kiddos and families. Way different ball game!

I think everyone should go to therapy at least once and if I could scream that to the world I would. I’m goofy. I tell it like it is. I like to laugh at myself. I don’t take myself too seriously. I don’t even come close to knowing everything about the world of mental health, but I do know more than your average person...so I feel obligated to help start conversations and answer your questions about mental health issues! My thought is that maybe by having these conversations, people won’t think it’s so scary to ask for help and will hopefully have some newfound tools to do so. Thanks for being here!
